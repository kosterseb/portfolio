From Kitchen to Code

Career Journey Skills & Experience

My professional journey began in the culinary world, where I spent 9 years honing my craft in high-pressure kitchen environments. As a head chef at Frederiksborg Castle, I learned valuable lessons about precision, teamwork, and creative problem-solving—skills that translate surprisingly well to web development.

Today, I'm studying web development at RTS in Denmark, where I'm focusing on combining clean, functional code with thoughtful design. My culinary background has given me a unique approach to development—I see coding as another form of craftsmanship, where attention to detail and the user experience are paramount.
