No Exit

Theater Music Score & Sound Design

A deeply immersive 4-month project creating the complete soundscape for Jean Paul Sartre's existential masterpiece "No Exit" (Lukkede Døre). This project challenged me to translate complex philosophical themes into an auditory experience that enhances the audience's emotional connection to the story's psychological intensity.

Creative Elements

  • Original ambient soundscapes reflecting psychological tension
  • Layered audio design creating claustrophobic atmosphere
  • Dynamic sound cues synchronized with dramatic moments
  • Existential themes translated into sonic experiences
  • Live theater integration and technical implementation
